AWS Graviton5-based M9g database instances are now generally available for Amazon RDS for PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MariaDB, delivering up to 30% better performance and up to 23% better price/performance over Graviton4-based instances. New 24xlarge and 48xlarge sizes offer up to 192 vCPU, up to 100Gbps networking, and up to 72Gbps EBS bandwidth, available in US East (N. Virginia, Ohio), US West (Oregon), and Europe (Frankfurt).
