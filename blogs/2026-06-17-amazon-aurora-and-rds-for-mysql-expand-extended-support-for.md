Amazon Aurora MySQL-Compatible Edition and Amazon RDS for MySQL now offer Extended Support for MySQL 5.7 through June 30, 2029, extended from the previous February 28, 2027 end date. This applies to Aurora MySQL version 2 and RDS for MySQL version 5.7, with continued critical security patches and bug fixes at Year 3 pricing and no price increase, available in all Regions where these services run.
