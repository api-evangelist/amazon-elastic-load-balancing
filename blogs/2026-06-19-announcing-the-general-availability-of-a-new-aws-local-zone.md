AWS launched a new Local Zone in Hanoi, Vietnam (ap-southeast-1-han-1a), supporting Amazon EC2 C7i/M7i/R7i instances, Amazon S3 One Zone-IA, Amazon EBS Local Snapshots, ECS, EKS, VPC, AWS Direct Connect, and Application Load Balancer. It is one of the first AWS Local Zones in Asia Pacific with S3 and EBS Local Snapshot support, helping customers meet data residency requirements and achieve single-digit millisecond latency.
