AWS Compute Optimizer now analyzes two additional CloudWatch metrics, VolumeIOPSExceededCheck and VolumeThroughputExceededCheck, to give better visibility into IOPS and throughput spikes when delivering Amazon EBS volume rightsizing recommendations. Available in all Regions where Compute Optimizer is offered except GovCloud (US) and China, with the underlying CloudWatch metrics at no additional charge for EBS volumes on Nitro-based instances.
