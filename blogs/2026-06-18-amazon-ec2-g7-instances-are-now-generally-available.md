Amazon EC2 G7 instances, accelerated by NVIDIA RTX PRO 4500 Blackwell Server Edition GPUs, are now generally available, delivering up to 4.6x AI inference performance and up to 2.1x graphics performance versus G6. They feature up to 8 GPUs with 32 GB memory each, custom Intel Xeon 6 processors, and up to 700 Gbps EFA networking, available in US East (Ohio) and US West (Oregon).
