Nested virtualization is now available on additional Intel instance platforms including C7i, R7i, M7i, C8id, R8id, M8id, C7i-flex, M7i-flex, I7i, C8i-flex, R8i-flex, M8i-flex, and X8i, plus US GovCloud (US-East) and US GovCloud (US-West). Customers can run KVM or Hyper-V on virtual EC2 instances for use cases like mobile emulators, in-vehicle hardware simulation, and Windows Subsystem for Linux.
