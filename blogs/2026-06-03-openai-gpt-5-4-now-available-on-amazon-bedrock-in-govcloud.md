Amazon Bedrock now supports OpenAI GPT-5.4 in AWS GovCloud (US-West), enabling government and regulated industry customers to use the model for complex reasoning, coding, document analysis, and multi-step workflows. Security and governance controls ensure prompts remain in AWS environments, meeting strict compliance requirements for government workloads.
