Amazon Aurora PostgreSQL, Aurora DSQL, and Amazon DynamoDB are now available on Vercel Marketplace across additional AWS Regions. Users can create databases in seconds under a new AWS account or link to an existing one, with new accounts receiving $100 USD in credits for up to six months.
