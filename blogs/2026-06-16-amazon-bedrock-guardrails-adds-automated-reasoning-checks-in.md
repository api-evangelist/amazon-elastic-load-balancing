Automated Reasoning checks in Amazon Bedrock Guardrails, which use formal verification to validate AI model outputs with up to 99% accuracy in detecting correct responses, are now available in the Asia Pacific (Sydney) Region. The capability helps address hallucinations and policy compliance for regulated industries, joining existing availability in US East (N. Virginia, Ohio), US West (Oregon), and Europe (Frankfurt, Ireland, Paris).
