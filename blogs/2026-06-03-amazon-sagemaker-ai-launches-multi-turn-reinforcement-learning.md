Amazon SageMaker AI launches multi-turn reinforcement learning, a new serverless model customization technique for fine-tuning models on multi-step, agentic tasks. The offering trains models against custom agent environments with built-in MLflow tracking and supports multiple foundation models for AI capability enhancement.
