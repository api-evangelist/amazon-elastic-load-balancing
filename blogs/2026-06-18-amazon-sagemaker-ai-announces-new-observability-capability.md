Amazon SageMaker AI's new observability capability gives visibility into token performance, GPU health, inference component placement, and autoscaling behavior, tracking metrics like Time to First Token, inter-token latency, queue depth, and tokens per second. A pre-built SageMaker AI Insights dashboard in CloudWatch surfaces these with OpenTelemetry-native metrics and no instrumentation, and customers can also connect Grafana via a regional PromQL endpoint. Available in 17 AWS Regions.
