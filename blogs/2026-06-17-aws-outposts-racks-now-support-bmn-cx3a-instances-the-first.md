date: "2026-06-17"

Second-generation AWS Outposts racks now support bmn-cx3a instances, featuring 5th Gen AMD EPYC processors at up to 4.1 GHz and NVIDIA ConnectX-7 NICs delivering up to 800 Gbps of bare-metal accelerated network bandwidth. They offer up to 256 cores and 1.5 TB memory across bmn-cx3a.metal-32xl and metal-64xl sizes with 2x 8 TB NVMe SSD, plus L2 multicast and hardware PTP support, available wherever second-gen Outposts racks are supported.
