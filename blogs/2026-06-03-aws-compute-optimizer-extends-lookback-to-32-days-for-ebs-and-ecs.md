AWS Compute Optimizer now extends lookback periods from 14 to 32 days for EBS volumes and ECS services at no additional cost. This longer period allows Compute Optimizer to account for monthly utilization patterns, such as month-end processing, when generating rightsizing recommendations for more accurate cost optimization.
