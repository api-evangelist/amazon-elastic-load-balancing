AWS HealthOmics now streams workflow engine logs to Amazon CloudWatch in real time, giving immediate visibility into workflow orchestration events, task scheduling, import/export activity, and full error stack traces during a run. Customers can set CloudWatch alarms on log patterns, build dashboards, and integrate observability tooling. Available for Nextflow, WDL, and CWL runs in all HealthOmics regions.
