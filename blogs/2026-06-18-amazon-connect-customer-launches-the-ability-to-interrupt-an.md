title: "Amazon Connect Customer launches the ability to interrupt an agent with an urgent contact"
url: "https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2026/06/amazon-connect-interrupt-agent-with-urgent-contact/"
date: "2026-06-18"
feed_url: "https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/recent/feed/"
---
Amazon Connect Customer now supports interrupting an agent with an urgent or time-sensitive contact, overriding their usual routing configuration so a call can ring even while the agent is on another call or in a custom status. This lets supervisors directly assign certain contacts to a specific agent. Available in all AWS regions where Amazon Connect Customer is offered.
