Amazon Redshift is expanding general availability of Graviton-based RG instances to Africa (Cape Town), Asia Pacific (Bangkok), and Mexico (Central), offering up to 4.2X better price-performance, running up to 2.4x faster than RA3 instances, and costing 30% less per vCPU. Customers can provision rg.xlarge and rg.4xlarge node types and upgrade existing RA3 instances, with built-in savings from incremental snapshots and elimination of Redshift Spectrum scanning charges.
