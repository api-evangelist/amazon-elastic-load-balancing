Amazon ECS service auto scaling now supports high-resolution 20-second metrics and metric publishing optimizations. In AWS benchmarks, time to trigger scale-out improved from 363 to 86 seconds (76% faster) and total time to scale and provision new tasks improved from 386 to 109 seconds (72% faster). Available across Fargate, ECS Managed Instances, and EC2 in all commercial and GovCloud (US) Regions.
