Amazon Bedrock AgentCore adds optimization capabilities that turn production traces into continuous improvement, surfacing failure, intent, and trajectory insights across hundreds of sessions to catch silent behavioral failures. Recommendations suggest specific system prompt and tool description changes, validated through batch evaluation and A/B testing on live traffic. Insights are in preview in 13 Regions; batch evaluations, recommendations, and A/B tests are generally available in 14 Regions.
